Privacy Policy

Brain Health Memory Game
Last Updated: December 2024
Important: This app is designed for users of all ages, including children under 13. We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of all our users, especially children.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Brain Health Memory Game ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Nickname: Display name chosen by the user
  • Date of Birth: Used to calculate age for brain health analysis
  • Gender: Optional demographic information for personalized experience
  • Country: Used for language localization and regional features
  • PIN Code: 2-digit code for multiplayer game verification

2.2 Game Data

  • Game scores and performance metrics
  • Brain health progress and statistics
  • Game completion times and difficulty levels
  • Multiplayer game session data
  • Achievement and progress tracking

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage analytics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Language and region preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core App Functions

  • Provide personalized brain health tracking and analysis
  • Enable multiplayer gaming features
  • Save and sync your game progress across devices
  • Display leaderboards and rankings
  • Provide age-appropriate content and difficulty levels

3.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features and game modes
  • Ensure app security and prevent abuse

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT sell your personal information

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (Firebase, Google Analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Anonymous Data: Aggregated, non-personal data for research and improvement purposes

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Authentication and user account management
  • Cloud database for game data storage
  • Analytics for app performance monitoring

5.2 Google AdMob

  • Displays advertisements within the app
  • May collect advertising identifiers
  • Follows Google's advertising policies for child safety

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Special Protection for Children Under 13

We take extra precautions to protect children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent
  • Children's accounts have additional privacy protections
  • We use child-safe advertising practices
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information by contacting us
  • We do not enable direct communication between users under 13

6.1 Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review what information we have collected from their child
  • Request that we delete their child's personal information
  • Refuse to permit further collection of their child's information
  • Contact us with questions about our privacy practices

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only
  • Secure cloud infrastructure provided by Google Firebase

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Game progress: Until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized after 26 months
  • Crash reports: Deleted after 90 days

9. Your Rights and Choices

9.1 Account Management

  • Access: View your personal information in the app settings
  • Update: Modify your profile information at any time
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us

9.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of non-essential notifications
  • Choose your preferred language and region settings
  • Control data sharing for analytics (where technically possible)

10. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Use the account deletion feature in the app
  • Visit our web portal at: alienpro.dothome.co.kr/deleteAccount
  • Contact us directly for assistance

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All game progress, brain health data, and personal information will be permanently removed from our systems.

11. International Users

Our app is available globally. By using our app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in countries where our service providers operate, including the United States. We ensure that all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
